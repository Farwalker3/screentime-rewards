Privacy Policy

App name: Screentime Rewards
Package: us.kodair.allowanceally
Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Overview

Screentime Rewards ("the App") is a family tool that lets parents set screen-time goals for their children and reward them with allowances when those goals are met. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children.

2. Information We Read From Your Device

The App requests the Usage Access permission (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS) to read your device's daily screen-time statistics. This information is:

  • Read locally on your device only.
  • Used solely to display your screen time and compare it against your goal.
  • Never transmitted to any external server, third party, or cloud service.

3. Account Information

The App currently uses local device storage for all account and goal data. We do not collect, store, or transmit email addresses, passwords, or personal profile information to any server.

4. Children's Privacy

Screentime Rewards is designed to be used by parents on behalf of their minor children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The child-facing portion of the app displays only screen-time data that is already present on the child's own device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any information to third parties. No analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services are included in the App.

6. Data Retention

All goal and balance data is stored locally on the device. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as new features are added (for example, cloud sync). The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
